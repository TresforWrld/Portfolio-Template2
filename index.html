Understanding Outer Space

What is Space?

Space is the vacuum beyond Earth's atmosphere, filled with planets, stars, satellites, nebulae, galaxies, asteroids, meteoroids, comets, and cosmic dust.

Difference: Space vs Galaxy

Unlike space itself, a galaxy is a massive collection of stars, gas, and dust bound by gravity. Galaxies come in many shapes: elliptical, lenticular, spiral, dwarf, and irregular.

Green Pea Galaxy

This rare galaxy type was discovered in 2007 during the Sloan Digital Sky Survey. It appears small and bright green in survey images, hence the name “Green Pea.”
